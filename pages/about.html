LUBA: Web Antiframework

LUBA, a unique web antiframework, introduces a refreshing approach to web development by combining Lua and Bash scripting. Unlike traditional frameworks, LUBA embraces flexibility, allowing developers to create tailored architectures. Lua's simplicity and Bash's robustness form a potent combination, making LUBA an ideal choice for lightweight, efficient, and customizable web applications. With a growing community and a gentle learning curve for Lua and Bash enthusiasts, LUBA opens doors to a new paradigm in web development. Explore the possibilities and unleash the power of LUBA for your next project.
